AMBA Membership

AMBA’s global membership is composed of MBA, MBM and DBA students and graduates the top 3% of Business Schools in the world.

By joining AMBA, you will instantly be part of an international, professionally diverse network of more than 35,000 members. From those in their first week of study, to experienced and senior business leaders, this buoyant network harnesses the collective expertise of the MBA community to share insights and thought leadership, create business networking opportunities and amplify the collective voice of MBAs all around the world.

Who should join?

AMBA Membership is available to all current students and graduates of AMBA-accredited programmes. Before beginning your application for membership, please make sure that your school/programme is accredited by AMBA.
